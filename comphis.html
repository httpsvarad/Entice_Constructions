COMPANY HISTORY

2020 / The Birth of an idea

Entice Constructions was conceived in 2022 when a passionate and forward-thinking person, led by Mr. Awadhnarayan Shukla, recognized the need for a fresh approach to construction. They believed that by combining cutting-edge technology, sustainable practices, and a dedication to craftsmanship, they could create something truly remarkable.

2023 / Laying the Foundation

With a clear vision in mind, Awadhnarayan Shukla,and his Son embarked on their entrepreneurial journey. They meticulously planned their entry into the construction market, securing initial funding, and assembling a talented team of labours, architects, engineers, and project managers who shared their passion for innovation.

2023 / The First Breakthrough

Entice Constructions made its debut by taking on a modest Metro line project in Dahisar, Mumbai. This early project will showcase the company's commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. The successful completion of this project will laid the groundwork for future growth.

Embracing Technology

Entice Constructions quickly embraced the latest construction technologies, from Building Information Modeling (BIM) to project management software. These tools streamlined processes, improved project efficiency, and set the company apart from competitors.

Navigating Challenges

Like any startup, Entice Constructions faced its share of challenges. Economic downturns, competition, and project setbacks tested the team's resilience. However, they persevered, learning valuable lessons along the way.

Building a Bright Future

Today, Entice Constructions stands as a promising player in the construction industry. With a portfolio of successful projects, a growing team of experts and a commitment to innovation and sustainability, the company looks to the future with boundless optimism.
